About

Lucid Lip brings together elements of multiple decade-spanning genres into a sound they call Alternative Rock and Soul. Throw together smokey yet smooth vocals floating over crisp, tight grooves, sensationally powerful guitars along with some heavy synthesizers and a wall of melodic harmonies, and you have the melting pot of vibe and sound that is Lucid Lip.
